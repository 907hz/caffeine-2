"Let's look into the causes and effects of caffeine"

It is one of the purine bases. It is the second most common ingredient in tea leaves after catechins, and tea contains about 2-4%. There are awakening, excitement, and diuretic effects, and tea is drunk on mankind as a favorite drink because of the refreshing feeling of caffeine.
